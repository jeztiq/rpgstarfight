Game Tips
  • Use LEFT/RIGHT arrows or touch buttons to move.
  • Press SPACE or FIRE to shoot enemies (1 shot for non-shooters, 2 for shooters).
  • Do not shoot green civilians — the game ends if you do.
  • Avoid 6 enemy shots — they end the game.
  • Keep fewer than 5 enemies past your line.
  • Speed increases after 10 kills.
Control

Touch controls appear on phones. Use the side controls to move and fire quickly.
